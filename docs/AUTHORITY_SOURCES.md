## SSA: Disability & Work

- **Work Activity Report (Employee - Form SSA-821)**  
  https://www.ssa.gov/forms/ssa-821.pdf
- **Work Activity Report (Self-Employed - Form SSA-820)**  
  https://www.ssa.gov/forms/ssa-820.pdf
  - *Note:* S-Corp officers often need to be familiar with both, as SSA may evaluate "Worth of Work" using 820 concepts despite W-2 status.
- **CDR (Continuing Disability Review) Overview**  
  https://www.ssa.gov/benefits/disability/work.html
- **SGA (Substantial Gainful Activity) - 2026 Limits**  
  https://www.ssa.gov/oact/cola/sga.html
  - *Note:* Specifically look for the "Blind" column.
- **TWP (Trial Work Period) - 2026 Thresholds**  
  https://www.ssa.gov/oact/cola/twp.html
- **POMS: Trusts (General Overview & Resources)**  
  https://secure.ssa.gov/poms.nsf/lnx/0501120200
  - *Note:* SI 01120.200 is the primary definition source for Trusts, even for SSDI cases regarding income assignment.
- **POMS: Corporate Officers & Business Structures (RS 02101.500+)**  
  https://www.google.com/search?q=https://secure.ssa.gov/poms.nsf/lnx/0302101500
  - *Note:* Explicit guidance on how SSA evaluates corporate officers' services and S-Corp/SSA distinctions.
